PRIVACY POLICY – FormaG

Last updated: December 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FormaG (“Application”) handles user information.
We take your privacy seriously and want to make it clear that we do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

1. Data Collection

FormaG does not collect any personal data from the user.
We do not request your name, email, phone number, location, photos, or any other identifiable information.

2. Permissions Used

The application only uses the permissions strictly necessary for its functionality:

2.1 Camera

Used exclusively to allow the user to capture images within the app.
Photos taken are not sent to any server and are not accessed by third parties.

2.2 Storage

Used only to allow the user to save or access PDF files or images created within the app.
No locally stored information is collected by the developer.

3. Local Storage

PDF reports and other files generated by FormaG are stored only on the user’s device.
The application does not send, share, transmit, or sync these files with any server.

4. Third-Party Services

FormaG does not use any third-party services that collect data, such as:

Firebase

Google Analytics

AdMob

Crashlytics

External servers

Therefore, no data is transmitted to third parties.

5. Login and Accounts

FormaG does not have login functionality and does not require any type of user registration.

6. Security

Since the app does not collect or transmit personal information, the risk of unauthorized access is minimal.
Even so, we recommend that users keep their devices updated and secure.

7. Children

The app may be used by minors, as it does not collect personal data and does not display inappropriate content.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally.
The latest version will always be available via the link provided in the Google Play Store listing.
