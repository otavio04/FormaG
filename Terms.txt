## **3. Technical Data Collection, Synchronization, and Use**

To enable advanced data analysis and continuous improvement of the tool, FormaG collects and processes specific technical data from the performed tests.

### **3.1 Scope of Collection**
The collected data consists **strictly of the exact same numerical information and images generated in the final report that is saved locally on the user's device**, accompanied by the precise timestamp (date and time) of the test creation. The application **does not collect** any user identity data, such as name, email, accounts, or precise geographic location.

### **3.2 Purpose and Cloud Storage**
The technical data and images are securely transmitted to our cloud repository (Firebase Storage) and will be used **exclusively for statistical studies, scientific research, and technical tool improvements** by the developers. None of the collected information is commercialized or shared with advertising networks.

### **3.3 Background Synchronization (Offline Support)**
The application features built-in offline synchronization managed by the Android system. If a user finalizes a test without a network connection, a temporary encrypted copy is kept in the app's private cache. As soon as a stable connection is detected, the upload is completed in the background, and the cache copy is immediately deleted, preserving the phone's storage. The public PDF report stored in the user's "Documents" folder is **never** deleted or altered by the application.
